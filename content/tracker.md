# Symptom Tracker

Keeping a symptom diary is one of the most effective things you can do before and between doctor visits. A clear record of pain levels, timing, and associated symptoms helps your specialist identify patterns, choose the right tests, and adjust treatment faster.

**How to use this tool:** Log your symptoms daily or whenever they change. Record pain intensity, location, mood, and any notes. Over time, the history chart reveals patterns you might not notice otherwise - \1ike flares tied to your cycle, stress, or diet.

**Your privacy:** All data stays on your device in local storage. Nothing is sent to any server, ever. You can export your history as a file to share with your doctor if you choose to.
