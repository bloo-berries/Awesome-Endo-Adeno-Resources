## In Memory

Endometriosis is not "just a bad period." It is a systemic disease that can affect every organ, erode quality of life, and - in severe cases - contribute to premature death. This page honors those who lived with Endometriosis, spoke openly about it, and whose voices continue to drive change even after their passing.
