# Privacy

This site is built to handle your data with care. Symptom information is sensitive, and we treat it that way.

## What stays on your device

Everything below lives only in your browser's `localStorage`. It is **never transmitted** to any server, never read by us, and never shared with third parties.

| What | Where |
|---|---|
| Symptom quiz answers (current session) | In-memory only; cleared when you navigate away |
| Saved quiz results (if you opt in) | `localStorage` key `quiz-result` |
| Symptom tracker entries | `localStorage` key `tracker-entries` |
| Theme preference (light/dark) | `localStorage` key `theme` |
| Language preference | `localStorage` key `site-language` |
| Sidebar expand/collapse state | `localStorage` key `sidebar-groups` |

You can clear all of this at any time using your browser's "Clear site data" option, or via the **Clear my data** button below.

## What we send to third parties

By default: **nothing**.

The only third-party submission on the site is the optional "help us improve" form at the end of the symptom quiz, sent to [Formspree](https://formspree.io). That form is **opt-in only** &mdash; you must check a clearly-labelled consent box to submit. Without that checkbox, no data leaves your device.

## Fonts and analytics

- **Fonts:** The Figtree typeface is self-hosted from this domain. We do not load fonts from Google Fonts or any other third-party CDN, so your IP is not shared with font providers.
- **Analytics:** We do not run any analytics tracker (no Google Analytics, no Plausible, no Fathom, no Heap).
- **Cookies:** This site sets no cookies of any kind.

## Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with anyone. Because we do not collect data in the first place, there is nothing to sell.

## Your rights under GDPR / CCPA

You have the right to know what data we hold about you, to have it deleted, and to opt out of any processing. Because we do not store data on our servers, this is straightforward: clear your browser's site data and the slate is wiped clean.

If you submitted the optional Formspree feedback form, you can request deletion of that submission by emailing the address listed in [About](/about/).

## Children

This site contains medical information that may not be appropriate for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

## Changes to this notice

If we change anything that affects what data is collected or how it's used, the change will be flagged at the top of this page for at least 90 days, and the date below will be updated.
